OF CHANALL

THE ORIGINS

CHANALL was founded in 2026 by Madame Chanall in a small apartment in Paris — or perhaps Brooklyn. The exact location is deliberately ambiguous, as is the nature of the brand itself.

Frustrated by the exclusivity of luxury fashion, Madame Chanall set out to prove a radical thesis: that the language of elegance belongs to everyone, and that a black and white color palette can make anything look expensive.

THE PHILOSOPHY

At CHANALL, we believe in the democratization of desire. Our products are not simply household goods — they are objets de nécessité, elevated by context and intention.

When you purchase a CHANALL instant noodle, you are not buying sustenance. You are acquiring a lifestyle. You are making a statement. You are telling the world: I understand irony.

Our signature double-C monogram — distinguished from all other double-C monograms — appears on each product as a mark of our commitment to accessible absurdity.

THE CHANALL STORY

1883

The Founding Vision

A woman dreams of a world where garbage bags come in matte black with gold lettering. This woman does not exist yet, but the dream does.

1921

The Iconic Fragrance

CHANALL No. 5 is not a perfume. It is a dish soap. Available at your local convenience store. A revolution in sensory experience.

1955

The Signature Bag

The 2.55 — named for its price in cents. A quilted trash liner with a revolutionary chain handle. Functional. Iconic. Holds approximately 13 gallons.

2026

The Digital Era

CHANALL launches its official website. The world finally has access to the finest instant noodles ever described using the word provenance.

THE CHANALL VALUES

01

ACCESSIBILITÉ

Everything CHANALL creates is available at your local supermarket. We believe luxury should not require a waitlist — unless the cashier line is very long.

02

AUTHENTICITÉ

CHANALL products are authentically cheap. We make no apologies for this. We consider it the highest form of transparency in an industry built on illusion.

03

DURABILITÉ

Our trash bags are rated for up to 15 kg of household waste. Our cotton swabs last until they are used. This is the CHANALL promise.
